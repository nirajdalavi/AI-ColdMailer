# Privacy Policy — AI ColdMailer

**Last updated:** June 2025

AI ColdMailer is a Chrome extension that helps you write and send job application emails.

## What we collect

**Nothing is sent to our servers.** This extension has no backend. All data stays on your device unless you explicitly send an email via Gmail or call OpenRouter with your own API key.

### Stored locally on your device

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Resume PDF | Attached when you send emails |
| Extracted resume text | Used to generate tailored emails |
| OpenRouter API key | Generates email drafts (stored in `chrome.storage.local`) |
| Application history | Shows your past sent applications |
| Gmail connection status | Whether Gmail is linked for sending |

### Sent to third parties (only when you take action)

| Service | What is sent | When |
|---------|--------------|------|
| **OpenRouter** | Your resume text + job description | When you click "Generate Email" |
| **Gmail (Google)** | Email content + resume attachment | When you click "Send via Gmail" |

We do not operate OpenRouter or Gmail. Their privacy policies apply to that data.

## What we do not collect

- No analytics or tracking
- No accounts on our servers
- No selling of data
- No access to your browsing history (page text is only read when you click "Use Current Page")

## Data retention

Data remains in your browser until you uninstall the extension or clear extension storage. Uninstalling removes all locally stored data.

## Your API keys

Your OpenRouter API key is stored only in `chrome.storage.local` on your machine. It is never transmitted to us.

## Children's privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.

## Contact

Open an issue on the project's GitHub repository or contact the publisher via the Chrome Web Store listing.
